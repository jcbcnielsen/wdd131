Welcome!

The Fredericksburg Historical European Martial Arts Club is a club in downtown Fredericksburg, Virginia for learning and practicing historical European combat techniques for both intellecutal and sporting purposes.

We love to teach almost as much as we love to learn, so we provide basic instruction for free to members and practical lessons for a modest fee. In addition to lessons, we also provide gym space for members to practice and spar with each other. We hold a tournament once a year in the spring, so be sure to practice your footwork and techniques in preperation if you wish to participate!

Finally, while bringing your own weapons and armor is encouraged, we have rental equipment available for those who don't wish to bring their own. Please note that while we strive to aquire enough rental equipment to meet the needs of our members, high demand or unusually sized armor may limit availability.

What is HEMA?

HEMA stands for Historical European Martial Arts. While technically covering a much wider span of history and practices, the majority of enthusiasts and practitioners focus on armed combat techniques from the Late Middle Ages and Renaissance, and fencing from the Modern period. HEMA is both a study of history and a combat sport similar to modern fencing.
