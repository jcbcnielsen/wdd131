Site Plan

Name:

FXBG HEMA

Reason: it is an abbreviation of the descriptive name "Fredericksburg Historical European Martial Arts"

Purpose:

Provide a hub for information and the ability to sign up for a HEMA club in the Fredericksburg area

Scenarios:

What is HEMA?

What equipment do I need to participate?

How do I get in contact with club leadership?
